Privacy Policy Effective October 10, 2024
This Privacy Policy describes Check Out My Budget's information practices for its browser extension. Check Out My Budget is a Chrome extension that allows users to view their budget, which they manage by a separate website, while browsing on other websites. Check Out My Budget takes your privacy seriously and want you to understand what information is stored by the extension and what it is used for.
What does Check Out My Budget store?
Check Out My Budget stores your budget information locally after authenticating with YNAB but this information does not leave your device. The information stored locally includes:
  • The budget information pulled from YNAB including the budget name, budget category names, and their balance.
  • Your preference for which budget items to display and how to display them per website.
  • Account information needed to request updated budget information from ynab, called authentication tokens.
How do we use the information stored?
The information stored locally is only used for the updated display of budget information on the select set of e-commerce websites.
Information Sharing
Check Out My Budget will not share your information with any third parties with the following exceptions:
  • With your explicit consent
  • When required by law
